# Problem Statement

Students often know what they must study but struggle to turn a broad syllabus into a realistic daily routine. Fixed timetables ignore different exam dates, confidence levels, weak subjects, and available time. The result is procrastination, uneven revision, and anxiety near exams.

StudyPilot AI translates a learner profile into a prioritized plan, daily tasks, revision phases, and motivational guidance. Its four logical agents separate planning, revision, analytics, and motivation so each decision has a clear purpose. The included MockGraniteAgent works entirely offline, making the tool accessible where cloud AI access is limited; the IBM Granite adapter offers a direct path to richer production inference later.
